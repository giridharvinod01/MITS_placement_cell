The Muthoot Group, with a reputation that has been shaped over decades with high quality practices, total customer satisfaction and steady growth, spanning 130 years in the field of business, is a legacy built on God-given values of trust, truth, transparency and tradition and has become one of the top business houses today by the grace of God. Muthoot takes its name from its Family, which is a branch of a traditional Orthodox Christian family, based in Kozhencherry, a small town in the erstwhile primary state of Travancore (Kerala). In the year 1887, Muthoot Ninan Mathai (Patron Founder of the group), started in a humble way as a retail and whole-sale trader of grains at Kozhencherry. The family partition took place in the year 1979 which led to the Genesis of Muthoot George Group (MGG). The story of The Muthoot Group is built on the foundations of passion, commitment, honesty, traditions and values.
